BIOMES
========================

Desert:
 - Low to medium altitude
 - No humidity
 - High temperature

Tundra:
 - Low to high altitude
 - Low humidity (during summer)
 - Low temperature

Forest:
 - Low to high temperature
 - low to high humidity
 - Medium to high altitude

Grassland:
 - Medium to high temperatures
 - Medium humidity
 - Medium altitudem
